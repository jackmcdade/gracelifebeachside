{{ svg src="assets/icons/music-note-2.svg" class="" }} The Worship {{ svg src="assets/icons/music-note-1.svg" class="rotate-5" }}

If you want to judge us based on our music — We have drums, keys, guitars, a cello, bass, and sometimes even a harmonica. But more important than the style of music — we're here to worship together, not to put on a performance.

{{ svg src="assets/icons/rocking-horse.svg" }} Kids Ministry {{ svg src="assets/icons/slide.svg" }}

During our Sunday morning service, we have a nursery for the littles and Sunday School classes for the bigs — up thru 5th grade. We love our kids. For all the older kids, they can (and do) enjoy the main service. Our teaching is great for all ages.

{{ svg src="assets/icons/hands-grasp.svg" }} Small Groups {{ svg src="assets/icons/group-read.svg" }}

We call 'em "Care Groups" because they're the primary way we care for each other as a church. Christianity is a team sport — we're not meant to do this alone. We have groups that meet in homes all over the Ormond Beach area, from beachside to west of I-95.

{{ svg src="assets/icons/hand-world.svg" }} Missions {{ svg src="assets/icons/trip-pins.svg" }}

We have to primary mission fields. First, to the local community through our counseling ministry, The Harbor and secondly to the people of Marabella, Costa Rica through on-the-ground work & years of building relationships.
