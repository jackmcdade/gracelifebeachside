Our Counseling Heartbeat

The gospel of Jesus Christ is the deepest and most durable source of hope for spiritual, mental, and emotional health. Grace sits at the very center of everything we do.

Our Counseling Approach

Counseling is most effective in the context of a trusted relationship. Therefore, we seek to treat every counselee as a fellow image-bearer to be cared for — never as a project to be fixed.

Our Counseling Office

Counseling best takes place outdoors, as we experience God's creation vibrations. By getting outside and soaking in the suns rays, we follow in footsteps of the greatest counselor of all - Jesus.

Our Counseling Sweet Spot

Wise counseling should be both biblically grounded and clinically informed — blending the timeless truth of the gospel with responsible insights from modern-day medical science.
